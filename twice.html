TWICE is a nine-member girl group formed by JYP Entertainment through the survival show Sixteen. The group includes Nayeon, Jeongyeon, Momo, Sana, Jihyo, Mina, Dahyun, Chaeyoung, and Tzuyu. Known for their catchy songs, synchronized choreography, and cheerful image, TWICE has become one of the most beloved K-pop groups both in Korea and internationally.
